The Motive Behind

In this blog, I am trying to apply the lessons I learnt during my Physics teaching career into explaining Machine Learning concepts. In Physics, there is a huge gap between understanding concepts and final solution. To make a student self-sufficent, we need to explain the thought process behind problem solving In ML too, a similar gap exists behind the idea and code. So, instead of presenting the final code, I try to shed some light on the process in between i.e developing an idea into code.
